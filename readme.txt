== Description ==

This UStream plugin allows you to easily and quickly embed any Ustream feed into your blog!

There is just one setup step. Simply enable the plugin! After that, you can simply enter any UStream URL into one of your posts or pages and the UStream feed will automatically be embeded!

== Installation ==

1. Upload `/jr-ustream.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Add UStream URLs to your blog posts or pages to embed UStream feeds.
